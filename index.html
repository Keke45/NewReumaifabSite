About Us

REUMAIFAB TECH & Marketing Services Limited is a Zambian-based technology and marketing private company established in 2019. From the early stages of operation, we’ve gained vast experience through diverse projects across various sectors.

We believe in quick delivery of the best results — every time. Our workflow is streamlined for maximum efficiency, ensuring we meet and exceed client expectations.

Research is at the core of everything we do. We explore and test ideas thoroughly before developing them into real-world products and solutions. And when challenges arise, we rely on a strong network of experienced associates always ready to step in.

As a growing conglomerate in response to rising demand for tech solutions, we’ve expanded into multiple sectors — technology, media, and digital marketing — through platforms like Reumaifab.com, Shop Africa, and Chrisma eMarketers.

Innovation. Speed. Excellence. That’s the Reumaifab way.
